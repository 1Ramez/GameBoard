Detailed Description

Represents the board for the Diamond Tic-Tac-Toe variant.

This game uses a diamond-shaped 7×7 grid where only specific cells are playable. Players aim to win by simultaneously forming a line of three and a line of four in different directions (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal). The two lines may share one common cell.

Invalid (non-playable) cells are marked in the board and cannot be selected.
