Detailed Description

Implements the board logic for Misere (Reverse) Tic-Tac-Toe.

In Misere Tic-Tac-Toe, players try not to make a 3-in-a-row. The first player who forms a winning line actually loses.

Board size is 3×3, and the game ends when:

  • A player forms a 3-in-a-row (they lose).
  • All 9 cells are filled with no losing lines (draw).
